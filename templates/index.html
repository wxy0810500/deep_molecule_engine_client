ADMET, i.e. Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, Excretion and Toxicity, is one of the import domains in drug R&D. Based on the historic experimental data, AI make it possible to assess ADMET properties virtually and early in drug pipelines.

Combining different drug pipeline knowledge, more than 60 important and common ADMET properties (Fig. 1) were constructed with deep learning AI models, with the good performance of AUC *~0.97 in 5-fold cross validation (Fig. 2). This service helps to automatically profile and systematically assess your own compounds’ ADMET properties by 1 click workflow (Fig. 3).
